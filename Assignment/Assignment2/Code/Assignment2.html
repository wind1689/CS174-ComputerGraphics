Instruction:

1. Press "C": Cycles the color of cubes & Show the cross hair;
2. Press "up"/"down": Control the altitude of camera;
3. Press "left"/"right": Control the azimuth of camera;
4. Press "I"/"J"/"K"/"M": Control forward, left, right and backward movement of camera;
5. Press "B": Reset all the view and animation of cube;
6. Press "N"/"W": Make the field of view narrower or wider;
7. Press "A": To start or stop the animation of rotating and scaling;
